##################################
Welcome to VideoF2B documentation!
##################################

Hello everyone!

`VideoF2B <https://videof2b.blogspot.com/>`_ is a **desktop application** that draws figures in videos of **Control Line F2B** stunt flights.

To get started, navigate the **table of contents** or follow one of these links:

.. image:: images/read_online.svg
    :target: https://videof2b.readthedocs.io/en/latest/installing.html
    :alt: `Read Online`
    :width: 20%

.. image:: images/download_pdf.svg
    :target: https://videof2b.readthedocs.io/_/downloads/en/latest/pdf/
    :alt: `Download PDF`
    :width: 20%

If you find any errors, omissions, opportunities for improvement, etc., let us know via email_ or GitHub_!

.. _email: mailto:videof2b.dev@gmail.com
.. _GitHub: https://github.com/alsolera/VideoF2B/issues
